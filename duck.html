Overview

I am working on a variant of Cops and Robbers, a pursuit-evasion game on graphs, inspired by the chess variant Duck Chess.

This project is still very much in progress, so I cannot share too much yet. That said, we have made a lot of progress, and I am saving the good stuff for the paper. More to come, I promise!
